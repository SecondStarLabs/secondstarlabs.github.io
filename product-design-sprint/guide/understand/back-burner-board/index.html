Exercise: Back-Burner Board

Capture 'back-burner' ideas that are good but not immediately relevant onto a sticky note board.

Back to Understand

Estimated time:

10 minutes

Supplies needed

  • White board
  • Markers
  • Post-its

Best to use when

A lot of ideas will be generated throughout the week. Some of the ideas will be pertinent to the tasks at hand, but others, although interesting, wonâ€™t be. Capture these good but not immediately relevant back burner ideas on a sticky note board during Converge.

Instructions

01.

Have a dedicated space to collect back burner ideas on the

whiteboard.
02.

Throughout the sprint have everyone write down ideas that are not directly solving the job-to-be-done and put them up on the whiteboard.

03.

As a team during the converge phase, review all the ideas that have been put up on the wall. Trash any that aren't relevant anymore.

04.

Record all ideas in a separate document or Trello.
