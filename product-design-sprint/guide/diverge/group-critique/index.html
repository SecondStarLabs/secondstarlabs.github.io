Exercise: Group Critique

In-depth discussion and critique of ideas.

Back to Diverge

Estimated time:

3 minutes for each stetch

Supplies needed

  • None

Best to use when

It is best to do a Group Critique after a Silent Critique has already been done. It allows for in-depth discussion around the idea. It gives time for people to say what they thought was good about the idea and for the person that had the idea to add any more detail to the thought.

Instructions

01.

Everyone gathers around a sketch or Story Board

02.

Ask people what they liked about it.

03.

Ask the person that had the idea to add any more explanation that they think hasn't been covered or if something was misunderstood.
