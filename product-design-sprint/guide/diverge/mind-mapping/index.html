Exercise: Mind Mapping

A warm up exercise to start generating ideas.

Back to Diverge

Estimated time:

10-15 minutes

Supplies needed

  • Paper
  • Sharpies

Best to use when

Mind Mapping is best used for personal ideas, thinking, notetaking, as a warm up to other exercises such as Crazy Eights. Individuals should use mind mapping to explore the problem however they choose. They shouldn't be shared with the group.

Instructions

01.

Hand out blank sheets of paper and sharpies to all participants.

02.

Set the timer for 10 minutes.

03.

Have everyone do quiet, individual brainstorming. Write or draw everything that they are thinking about. Keep the format free so that people feel comfortable to explore any and all ideas.
